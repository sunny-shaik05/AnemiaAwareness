6-Week Anemia Awareness Project Work

Week 1: Introduction to Anemia

In the first week, we introduced the concept of anemia, its types, and its global impact. We conducted awareness sessions in our college and distributed pamphlets. We also gathered baseline knowledge from participants through a short quiz. The team collaborated to design posters and plan the upcoming weeks.

Week 2: Causes and Risk Factors

We explored the biological and lifestyle causes of anemia. Sessions focused on iron deficiency, chronic diseases, and malnutrition. We interviewed healthcare professionals and created infographics to explain risk factors. Students shared personal stories, making the topic more relatable.
