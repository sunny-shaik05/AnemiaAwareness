How to Prevent Anemia

  • Eat iron-rich foods like spinach, lentils, and red meat.
  • Include vitamin C to help absorb iron better.
  • Take iron supplements if prescribed.
  • Get regular health checkups and blood tests.
  • Stay hydrated and maintain a balanced diet.

Common Symptoms of Anemia

Fatigue

Pale Skin

Dizziness

Shortness of Breath

Iron Deficiency
