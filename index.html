LET'S BEAT ANEMIA TOGETHER!!

RISKS


» Fatigue
» heart issues
» pregnancy complications
» reduced immunity are common risks of anemia.

PREVENTION & TREATMENT


» Iron-rich diet, supplements
» regular checkups
» treating underlying causes help prevent anemia.

SYMPTOMS


» Pale skin
» dizziness
» shortness of breath
» cold hands/feet are key symptoms to watch.

CAUSES


» Iron deficiency
» chronic diseases
» blood loss
» poor nutrition are major causes of anemia.
